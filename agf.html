AGF BASKETBALL

Website redesign for local basketball association
May 2023
5 weeks
Siiri Lietu, Claire Bisgaard, Paulius Burba, Natalia Sokolowska
WordPress, CSS, JavaScript

AGF Basketball original website lacks funcionality and professional design. During client research, we found out their goal of attracting more sponsors to support club's development.

We focused on improving data architecture and overall design to boost the professional look. Sponsors can find information tailored for their needs in a special subpage.

We built the solution using CMS to ensure relatively easy website management for volunteers associated with the club. Visit our website here.
