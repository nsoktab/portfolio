SMUKFEST FESTIVAL

Enhancement of app functionalities and design
March 2023
4 weeks
Yanitsa Tsoneva, Nina Zavodska, Samuel Hlousek, Natalia Sokolowska
Figma, Adobe Express, Premiere Pro

MinSmukfest App enhances the festival experience by allowing users to create friend groups, track their location and see communal beverage consumption. From our client we received task to refine existing functionalities and create design more coherent with the festival website.

We redesigned the app by adding illustrations provided by client and incorporating wavy style found on the main website. We expanded location funcionality by "Find my Tent" and "Create Event" options. My responsibility was to design "Beautiful Memories" section and create user flow for Smukfest Wrapped addition.
